How it works

From enquiry to fully insulated, in roughly six weeks.

Every project follows the same structured PAS 2035 retrofit pathway. Here's exactly what happens at each stage, who's involved, and what you can expect.

The technology

What goes behind your render.

External wall insulation adds a continuous thermal layer to the outside of your existing wall, behind a new render or brick finish. The original brickwork stays intact and protected.

The system is engineered to suit your property type. Most London Victorian and Edwardian terraces use a mineral wool or EPS board between 90mm and 120mm thick, depending on wall construction and required thermal performance.

The thermal layer works in both directions: it retains warmth indoors during winter and blocks solar heat gain through the walls during summer. London terraces typically run 3 to 5°C cooler internally during heatwaves after EWI, with the most noticeable difference on hot afternoons when uninsulated walls would normally radiate heat inward for hours.

Every system we specify carries British Board of Agrément certification with a 25-year warranty registered in your name and transferable on sale.

The process

Four stages. Clear handovers at each.

Every project follows the same path. We tell you exactly what happens, when, and who's involved.

i.

Free survey

Typically week 1. A PAS 2035 certified retrofit co-ordinator visits your property. They measure the elevations to be insulated, inspect wall construction, check for damp or structural issues, take internal moisture readings, and assess any planning or conservation considerations.

The survey takes 60 to 90 minutes. There's no charge and no obligation. You'll receive a full survey report within 48 hours covering the recommended system, expected thermal performance, and grant eligibility assessment.

ii.

Fixed-price quote

Within 48 hours of the survey. You receive an itemised, fixed-price quote: the total job cost, the grant amount you qualify for, your net contribution, and a clear timeline.

The figure on your quote is the figure you pay. Any genuine unforeseen issues (rare, but occasionally walls reveal surprises) are flagged and re-quoted before any additional work proceeds. You're never billed for something you haven't approved.

iii.

Grant administration

Weeks 2 to 6. If you qualify for ECO4 or the Great British Insulation Scheme, we handle the entire grant application process with the obligated energy company. You sign one document; we handle everything else.

Approval typically takes 2 to 6 weeks. We keep you updated at each stage. The grant funding is paid directly from the energy company to us, applied against your invoice. You never handle the grant money. Your invoice shows the grant amount applied and your net contribution.

iv.

Installation

Weeks 5 to 10. Scaffold up, system installed in stages: substrate preparation, mechanical fixings, insulation board, basecoat, mesh, render or finish, weather details. The standard sequence takes 2 to 4 weeks on site for a terraced property.

A PAS 2035 retrofit co-ordinator inspects the work at three defined stages and signs off final completion. You receive your BBA system warranty certificate, an as-built specification, and TrustMark project documentation in your customer pack.

After installation

Aftercare and long-term assurance.

The relationship doesn't end on the day the scaffold comes down.

  • 25-year BBA system warranty

    The insulation system carries British Board of Agrément certification covering material defects, thermal performance, and weathering for 25 years. Registered in your name, transferable on sale of the property.

  • 12 to 24-month workmanship guarantee

    We provide a workmanship guarantee covering any defects in installation quality. Aftercare is handled in-house, so you only ever deal with one team.

  • TrustMark dispute resolution

    Every project is registered with TrustMark, the government-endorsed quality scheme. If anything goes wrong and we can't resolve it directly, TrustMark provides independent dispute resolution at no cost to you.
