Why it matters

Why your Victorian terrace is freezing in winter and stifling in summer.

London's heritage stock predates modern insulation by a century. Solid 9-inch brickwork conducts heat freely in both directions: outward in winter, inward through July and August. The fabric is the problem, not the boiler or the fan.

35%

Heat lost through walls

An uninsulated solid-wall home loses roughly a third of its heat directly through external walls, more than any other single route.

5°C

Cooler in summer

The same insulation that retains heat in winter blocks solar gain in summer. Internal temperatures during heatwaves are typically 3 to 5°C lower in properly insulated homes.

£1,200

Average annual saving

A typical 3-bed Victorian terrace can expect £900 to £1,400 off annual heating bills after external wall insulation.

How it works

Four steps. Roughly six weeks from enquiry to install.

i.

Free survey

One of our PAS 2035 certified retrofit co-ordinators visits your property to assess wall construction, thermal performance, and grant eligibility. Typically within 7 days of your enquiry.

ii.

Fixed-price quote

Within 48 hours of your survey we send a clear, itemised quote: total cost, grant amount, your contribution. The figure on the quote is the figure you pay.

iii.

Grant administration

If you qualify for ECO4 or the Great British Insulation Scheme, we handle the entire application on your behalf. Approval typically takes 2 to 6 weeks.

iv.

Installation

Scaffold up, system installed, render finished, scaffold down. Typical 2 to 4 weeks on site for a terraced home. Backed by a 25-year BBA system warranty.
