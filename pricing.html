What affects price

Why the same property type can vary by thousands.

The packages above are typical figures. Your actual quote depends on factors specific to your property, identified during the survey.

Wall area to be insulated

Rear-only versus all elevations. A typical mid-terrace insulates rear only because front and party walls are protected. End-of-terrace and detached add side or front elevations.

Wall construction

Solid 9-inch brick is straightforward. Mixed construction (parts solid, parts cavity, parts timber-frame extensions) requires bespoke detailing and adds cost.

Finish choice

Standard silicone render is included. Brick-effect cladding, lime render for heritage properties, or decorative finishes add £3,000 to £10,000 depending on selection.

Access and scaffolding

Tight access alleys, narrow side returns, neighbour permissions, and bay window detailing all affect scaffolding requirements and labour time.

Existing condition

Damp, render failure, structural cracking, or failing pointing may need addressing before EWI. Our survey identifies these so the quote reflects reality, not assumption.

Planning considerations

Conservation areas, listed buildings, or properties facing the highway may require planning permission. We identify this at survey and quote accordingly.
